Text Alignment

Left, center, right, and justify

text-start

This text is aligned to the left side of its container.

text-center

This text is centered within its container.

text-end

This text is aligned to the right side of its container.

text-justify

This text is justified, meaning it stretches to fill the full width of its container with equal spacing between words on each line.
