Cover story

Inside the calm studio: how teams design for focus.

Magazine layouts let you vary story weight without losing structure. This lead feature anchors the grid and gives readers a clear starting point before the eye travels to secondary stories. We follow one studio's weekly ritual: a 15-minute reset, a single prioritized checklist, and a shared notes wall that replaces long status meetings. The result is a calmer handoff from planning to production, with fewer midweek surprises and clearer accountability.
